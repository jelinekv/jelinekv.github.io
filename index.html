Category Theory + Logic

Hi! I am a PhD student of Computer Science at the University of Sussex under the supervision of Alessio Santamaria and Bernhard Reus.
My research interests lie in various interactions between logic and category theory. By this, I mean mostly type theory (and its categorical semantics), and 2-dimensional structures (currently focused on getting a better understanding of contravariance and dinatural transformations).
